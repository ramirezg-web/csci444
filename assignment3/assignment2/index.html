Gentle's Big 5 Zodiac Placements

The word zodiac is used in astronomy and astrology to refer to the band of sky over which the sun, moon, and planets move–as seen from Earth, at least. The zodiac contains a number of constellations, and the sun (or other celestial body) is said to be “in” a constellation when it moves into the portion of the zodiac that includes that constellation.

In astrology, a sign of the zodiac refers to one of 12 specific constellations of the zodiac that the sun passes through.
